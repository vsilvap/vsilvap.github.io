Professional and Policy Experience

Dr Silva has contributed to major international research and policy projects, including UNDP studies on social development in Chile and Latin America, labour market assessments in Central America, global studies on platform work and refugee inclusion for the ILO and UNHCR, and regional analyses on labour and social policy responses to the Covid-19 pandemic under ECLAC. His work bridges academic research, policy design and international governance.
